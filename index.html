“Find your partner now!”.

Shiny hunting in Pokémon is the dedicated pursuit of finding rare, uniquely colored Pokémon, known as "shiny" Pokémon, which have a very low encounter rate compared to their standard counterparts.

  • A shiny Pokémon is a rare variant of a standard Pokémon with a distinct color palette, making it highly sought after by trainers due to its unique appearance and rarity.

    Read more >>

  • Shiny Pokémon are extremely rare, with the base odds of encountering one typically being 1 in 4,096 in recent games. Various methods and techniques, such as the Masuda Method, Shiny Charm, or chaining, can be employed to increase these odds, but they remain a prized and elusive find for most trainers.

  • Even Legendary Pokémon, which are typically unique and powerful Pokémon found in specific locations or through special events, can be shiny. The odds of encountering a shiny Legendary are the same as other Pokémon, but due to their unique encounter methods, the process can be more time-consuming
